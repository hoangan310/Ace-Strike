# Development Roadmap

## Phase 1: Core Polish (Current)
- [x] Modularize codebase.
- [x] Implement ship customization.
- [x] Add boss variety (4 types).
- [x] Refine HUD and Menus.

## Phase 2: Audio & Feedback (Next)
- [ ] **Sound System**: Implement a central audio manager.
  - [ ] Background music (BGM) for stages and bosses.
  - [ ] SFX for shooting, explosions, and power-up collection.
- [ ] **Screen Shake**: Add camera shake on player hit or boss explosion.
- [ ] **Visual Feedback**: Flash enemies white when hit.

## Phase 3: Content Expansion
- [ ] **Stage Backgrounds**: Move beyond simple stars to nebula clouds or planetary surfaces.
- [ ] **More Enemy Patterns**: Implement circular, homing, and formation behaviors for minions.
- [ ] **Secondary Weapons**: Add a "Bomb" or "Special" meter.
- [ ] **Achievements**: Track milestones like "Boss Slayer" or "Pacifist Run".

## Phase 4: Meta-Progression
- [ ] **Unlockables**: New ship shapes or colors tied to high scores.
- [ ] **Upgrade Shop**: Spend points earned in runs to permanently boost starting stats.
- [ ] **Story/Lore**: Add brief mission briefings between stages.
